Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes. Cancellations made before September 1, 2026 receive a full refund. After that date, we offer a 50% refund or a free transfer of your ticket to DevConf 2027.

Is there a student or academic discount?

Yes. Full-time students get 60% off the Standard pass with a valid .edu email or student ID. Email students@devconf2026.com after registering to apply the discount.

Will the talks be recorded?

All keynotes and breakout sessions are recorded. Standard passes include 30 days of digital access, while Pro passes include lifetime access to every recording.

What's included with a Team pass?

Team passes cover up to 10 attendees and include everything in Pro, plus a dedicated lounge, private workshop booking, your company logo on the event page, and a recruitment booth for one day.

Do I need to bring my own laptop for workshops?

Yes, please bring a laptop with your preferred code editor installed. Workshop materials and setup instructions will be emailed one week before the conference.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes. The venue is fully wheelchair accessible, with accessible restrooms, reserved seating in all session rooms, and live captioning available on request.
